About Me

I am currently working at Civica International as a Technical Analyst for School Management Systems. I have been in the role for 3 years in which time I was seconded for 9 months to write training material for the Financial module of the Civica Education Suite.

At present I am the lead support for iWise, a web-based School Management System, in this role I train and advise junior analysts and take all escalations. I have strong SQL knowledge along with a solid foundation in Jaspersoft reports.

My current role requires a strong grounding in SQL Query troubleshooting and resolving issues with Dataflow from third party applications.

Outside of work I enjoy gardening and playing golf every weekend at Maylands, my local course.
